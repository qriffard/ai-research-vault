# Stanford OVAL

The **Open Virtual Assistant Lab** at Stanford University, led by
[[monica-lam]]. Research focus on LLM-powered knowledge systems, virtual
assistants, and natural language interfaces.

## Key projects in this wiki

- [[storm]] — automated Wikipedia-like article generation (NAACL 2024)
- [[co-storm]] — collaborative knowledge curation (EMNLP 2024)
