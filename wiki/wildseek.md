# WildSeek

A benchmark dataset introduced alongside [[co-storm]] for evaluating complex
information-seeking assistance systems. Constructed from real user
information-seeking records collected on the public STORM website
(storm.genie.stanford.edu).

## Design

- Contains topics paired with user goals from real users engaged in
  multi-domain complex information seeking.
- Captures the dynamic, exploratory nature of real queries — no single gold
  query, but evolving goals.
- Used for automatic evaluation of both discourse trace quality and final report
  quality.
